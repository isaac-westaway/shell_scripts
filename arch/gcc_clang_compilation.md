## Compilation

### Catching errors

Works for both clang and clang++ \ 

```clang -Weverything -Werror -pedantic -pedantic-errors -o <compiled_output_file> <source_file>```

```gcc -Wall -Wextra -Werror -pedantic -pedantic-errors -o <compiled_output_file> <source_file>```
